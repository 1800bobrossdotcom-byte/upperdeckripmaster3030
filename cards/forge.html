How the forge works

Add cards you own as layers, then sculpt: drag to move, scale, pick a blend mode, and key out a color (eyedrop the art to punch it transparent) so the layer beneath shows through. Stack top, cut a face out of another, drop it on a third — frame it and mint. Forging trades the source cards in: 2–3 of them leave your collection and become house cards. Your forged card is yours to play. Every forge burns $UR3030. (preview — burns settle on-chain via the vault's forge())
