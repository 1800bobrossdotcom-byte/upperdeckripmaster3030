The Forge

key · load a brush · drag to paint · mint

Paint with the deck itself. Every part is a keyed-out cutout of a real card — a whole critter or a bit (a slice of one). Load one as a brush, then drag across the card to multiply it — paint a swarm, a wall, a halo of the same face. Switch to ✋ edit to drag, scale, spin, flip, blend or re-key any piece you laid down. Forging trades in the 2–3 cards you painted with; the forged card is yours. (the forge toll pays the creator)
