The Forge

strip · sticker · scramble · mint

The deck is stripped into parts. Flip through the drawers — critters (cards cut out of their backgrounds), backdrops, faces, FX and type — tap to drop a sticker on the card, then drag, scale, spin, flip, blend and key it. Hit scramble for a random mash-up, s to save a PNG. Forging trades in the 2–3 cards whose parts you used — they become house cards — and mints your new one, playable in the arena. (the forge toll pays the creator; see the binder to trade it)
