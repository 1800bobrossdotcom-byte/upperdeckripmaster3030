How the forge works

Pick 2 or 3 cards you own and slot them into the bands — the forge takes the top of one, the middle of another, the bottom of a third, and a frame of your choosing, and collages a brand-new card. Forging trades the inputs in: they leave your collection and become house cards the vault can re-issue. Your forged card is yours to play in the arena. Every forge burns $UR3030.
