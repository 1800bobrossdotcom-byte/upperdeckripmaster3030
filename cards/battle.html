The rules, such as they are

Every card counts its whole self: Σ = ATK + DEF. The weather comes live off the chain — one trigger amplified per ~25 blocks — and any card whose trigger matches it catches a ⚡ +4 amp into its Σ. That's the strategy: read the weather, build the stack. Wager like pogs — single, pair, trips, quad, or the all-in full slam of seven. Matching triggers inside your stack pay +3 a pair, going deep pays a size bonus. Highest total takes the pot and collects the loser's cards. And the stakes bite: you play what you've ripped — rip packs to get cards, stake them to win more, and a staked card is gone from your binder if you lose (in this prototype, all of it saved in your browser). No cards, no deal. Ties go to the house. The house always eats.
