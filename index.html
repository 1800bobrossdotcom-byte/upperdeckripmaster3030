How to play
Spec §1 · Fuel

1 Get the token

$UR3030 is the fuel. One liquid token on SuperRare's bonding curve — buy it, hold it, sell it back anytime. Everything below runs on it.

Rite §2 · Pack

2 Rip a pack

Seven cards for about $7 — the one premium move: buy ~350 $UR3030 on the curve and burn it all. Your pulls come off your burn tx. Each season's allotment dwindles, so packs get scarcer and pricier the deeper the game runs.

Rite §3 · The fire

3 Burn the token down

Every pack burns $UR3030 for good, so supply only falls — 3.03M → ~1.01M over the deck's life, a 3× contraction. The 100 cards all survive: nothing turns to ash. Rarity is set by community vote; the token burns so the art can live.

Rite §4 · Lenses

4 Every card is a lens

All 100 cards are live lenses (a render that reads the market). 33 hero cards mint as 1/1s — 11 pulled from packs, 22 earned in the games; the other 67 are render-only, mintable later. Own every copy of a field card and compress it into a 1/1. And every holder carries Lovebeing.

⚠ The house rule: on-chain there is exactly one token and one move — burn $UR3030. Packs burn in full, conviction burns, compression burns. No tolls, no fee wallet, no treasury, no other contract for one to hide in. Court votes & wagers are site-side signal; the fire is the only consensus.
Today the pack, binder, arena & companion cards are a testnet prototype — pulls and card ownership save in your browser, and on-chain cards ship in a later phase. The one thing that's real right now: the $UR3030 buy & burn.
