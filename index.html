How to play
Spec §1 · Fuel

1 Get the token

$UR3030 is the fuel. One liquid token on SuperRare's bonding curve — buy it, hold it, sell it back anytime. Everything below runs on it.

Spec §2 · Ballot

2 Burn to vote

Each season, candidate cards go up for a vote. Burning tokens is how you vote — the art you burn for gets printed into the season's pack. And the vote never closes: any holder can burn to promote or demote a printed card up and down the rarity ladder. Enough demotion and a card is voted off entirely. The deck is never final.

Spec §3 · Pack

3 Rip a pack

Burn the pack price, tear it open, get 7 cards. Odds run common to prizm — plus one sealed 1/1 marquee that no pack will ever cough up.

Spec §4 · Field use

4 Play the cards

Wager pog stacks in the arena — winner takes the loser's cards. Send them, trade them, collect them. Your cards, your call.

⚠ The one rule: every move burns $UR3030. No treasury, no fee wallet, no refunds — supply only goes down.
