How to play
Spec §1 · Fuel

1 Get the token

$UR3030 is the fuel. One liquid token on SuperRare's bonding curve — buy it, hold it, sell it back anytime. Everything below runs on it.

Rite §2 · Pack

2 Rip a pack

Seven cards for about $7 — the one premium move: buy ~350 $UR3030 on the curve and burn it all. Your pulls come off your burn tx. Each season's allotment dwindles, so packs get scarcer and pricier the deeper the game runs.

Rite §3 · The fire

3 Burn the field down

A season opens with the whole field alive and a published retirement queue — weakest first, everyone can read it. Every burn pushes the count; each milestone crossed turns the next card to ash in the render, down to the standard 77-card deck. Court votes and arena slams are the crowd's signal; burns are the consensus.

Rite §4 · Lenses

4 Deck, 1/1, or ash

Season's end, the 77 survivors mint as tradeable deck lenses — your serial of the card, sized to how few held it. Corner a card's whole claim set — survivor or retired — and compress it into a 1/1. The uncornered dead stay ash; final blows earn an Ash Trophy. Survive more seasons, gain provenance & stats.

⚠ The house rule: on-chain there is exactly one token and one move — burn $UR3030. Packs burn in full, conviction burns, compression burns. No tolls, no fee wallet, no treasury, no other contract for one to hide in. Court votes & wagers are site-side signal; the fire is the only consensus.
